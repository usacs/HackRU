Help Sponsor HackRU

USACS, the computer science club at Rutgers University, is an active student organization that is responsible for bridging the gap between technical students, faculty and the outside world at Rutgers University.

USACS will proudly be hosting HackRU for the fifth time! The event is a 24-hour hackathon. It will begin on October 12th at 1:00pm and conclude on October 13th at 5:00pm. There will be companies presenting their APIs so that students can explore them and create amazing applications. Check out pictures from HackRU this past spring .

USACS is integral in support a hacker culture on the Rutgers campus through mentorship, community, and friendship. Rutgers is a central hub for amazing and resourceful programmers. These students find their full-time jobs and summer internships through HackRU. By sponsoring the event, you are exposing yourself in a very positive light to a crowd with a potential employee or customer.

Help us make HackRU an incredible experience for learning and creation. You will find our sponsorship packages that explain how you can support HackRU. Please contact us at rutgersusacs@gmail.com if you have any questions.
